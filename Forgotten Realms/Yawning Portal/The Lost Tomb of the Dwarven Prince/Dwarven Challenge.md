# The Steadfast Crossing

At the end of the room is a 7' wide 12' tall corridor that extends out into the
darkness and curves to the right. Suspended from the ceiling is a 4" wide
"target" mounted on the end of a 2-inch wrought iron pole. The corridor slopes
downward at a 7-degree pitch and it's coated in slick grease, making standing
nearly impossible. On the floor of the corridor are sharp 3-inch tall spike and
periodic 7-inch tall blades.

Carved into the wall is: "Step once, and do not falter. Grimthölm grants passage
only to those who do not break faith with their stride."

There appears to be another pole protruding from the ceiling after 20'.


> If the players punch the target (AC8) with enough force (3 dmg) it will swing
> away and cause Grimthölm to pour from many holes in the ceiling which will drain
> away in 3 rounds. The ooze is thick and will not support any weight unless it
> is violently struct - such as caused by boots when running or stomping.

If the players run across the surface they will stay afloat; otherwise they will
sink to the bottom, and then fall over onto the spikes and take d2 damage if 
they fail vs. wand.

If they do not activate the Grimthölm and try to cross the greased floor, they
must roll d20 + 12 (or 20) <= DEX or fall, for every 5 crossed.


# The Burden of Stone

There is a 40' circular chamber with a railbed across it. On your side is a 
small deck with an open 4' tall clamshell enclosing an inclined press board. On
the press board is the skeleton of a dwarf with it's head smashed in.

Carved into the wall is: "Hold steady, the Mountain does not flinch."

> Laying down on the press board, with arms pulling the handles and legs
> pushing the plate, the character can safely cross. Releasing the handles,
> open the protective clam shells and exposes the player to fall rocks. Save vs.
> paralyzation or release the handles. The kick board requires 5 good strength
> rolls. Failed strength roll causes trolley to slip back to beginning by one
> press.


# The Hammered Ascent

A slender 3" chain hangs down form the ceiling into the middle of an iron 
platform. In the middle of the platform is a huge stone 'flywheel'. There are 
gears and levers connected to the wheel and platform. There is a drive gear with
three protruding levers at 120 degrees apart.

Carved into the wall, "Let your hammer speak until the stone obeys."

> Spinning the drive wheel fast enough and engaging the clutch will cause the
> platform to rise. The wheel must be struct (AC8) with 5 damage per round to keep
> the platform rising. Party must ascend 30' which take 10 rounds of consistent
> pounding.
