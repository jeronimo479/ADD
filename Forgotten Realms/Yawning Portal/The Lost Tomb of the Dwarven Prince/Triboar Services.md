# Wealth Storage Fees in Triboar (Speculative, Lore-Friendly)

***

## The Tower (Civic Authority)

**Type:** Government facility  
**Security Level:** High  
**Reputation:** Trustworthy, bureaucratic

### Likely Fees

*   **Account initiation:** 5-10 gp
*   **Monthly storage fees:**
    *   Small coffer (= 500 gp): 1 gp/month
    *   Medium chest (= 5,000 gp): 5 gp/month
    *   Large chest (= 20,000 gp): 10-15 gp/month
*   **After-hours withdrawal:** +2 gp
*   **Insurance:** 10% of stored value per year

### Reasons to Choose

*   Most secure place in town
*   Low corruption risk
*   Best for long-term storage

***

## Lionshield Coster

**Type:** Merchant consortium  
**Security Level:** High  
**Reputation:** Reliable, businesslike, profit-driven

### Likely Fees

*   **Account initiation:** Free for customers; 5 gp for non-customers
*   **Monthly storage fees:**
    *   Small (= 500 gp): 2 gp/month
    *   Medium (= 5,000 gp): 8 gp/month
    *   Large (= 20,000 gp): 20 gp/month
*   **Merchant bulk discount:** 10%
*   **Insurance options:**
    *   Basic (50% coverage): 5% of value/year
    *   Premium (full coverage): 15% of value/year
*   **Access fee:** 1 gp per access
*   **Default consequences:** Goods auctioned after 60 days unpaid

### Reasons to Choose

*   Professional handling
*   Insurance available
*   Convenient for merchants

***

## Everwyvern House

**Type:** Inn and caravan house  
**Security Level:** Medium  
**Reputation:** Honest but not a fortress

### Likely Fees

*   **Lockbox rental:**
    *   Small: 5 sp/day or 3 gp/week
    *   Large: 1 gp/day or 5 gp/week
*   **Adventurer trunk storage:** 10 gp/week
*   **Magic ward surcharge:** +1 gp/day
*   **Insurance:** Typically none
*   **Disclaimer:** Does not cover magical theft

### Reasons to Choose

*   Suitable for short-term storage
*   Convenient for travelers
*   Simple and accessible
