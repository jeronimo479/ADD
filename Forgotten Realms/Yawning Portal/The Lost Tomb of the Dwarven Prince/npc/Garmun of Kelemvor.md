# ? **Personality, Role, and Background (Narrative Section)**

### **Who Garmund Is**  
A middle-aged priest of **Kelemvor, Judge of the Dead**, Garmund is stern but compassionate. He has served as a funerary chaplain, monster hunter, and advisor to rural villages, including those near the Dessarin Vale. He has history with dwarves and great respect for their ancestral tombs.

### **Connection to Besilmer**  
Garmund has long studied the ancient kingdom of **Besilmer** and its funerary traditions. He sees the reopening of the prince’s tomb as a **cosmic disturbance** — a threat to the rightful peace of the dead.

- Speaks plainly; kindness wrapped in stoicism

### **Motivations**
